A green spark of energy whirls around its container of space, bouncing off walls, helplessly interacting with matter that comes its way. The energy reacts and interacts, connecting with other active agents in the world. Building and building, loop and looping.  

‘Metallic Forrest’ provides a mapping of interconnected systems, dissecting the interaction and mirroring of worlds. Layering of imagery provides a visual depiction of how multiplicity governs our worlds, helping us to deconstruct systems through the understanding of their essence. Through depicting the cyborgian nature of life systems, one can view themselves as part of a larger interconnected system that is constantly being effected, pushed and pulled, sparked by different energetical matters. 

Maybe once all systems are deconstructed, we are able to recognise our inherent alikeness and attunement to living begins around us, rebuilding from understandings of care and empathy. 

There always lies a between, a path of understanding and in between in each network, a space where transformation occurs. A transformation of constant becoming, ever flowing dynamic assemblage.
