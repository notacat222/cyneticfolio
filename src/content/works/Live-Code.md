Code Jam Residency FEB+MARCH’26:
Part workshop, part residency at Melbourne Electronic Sound Studio (MESS) lead by Nū and Melanie Huang to explore using code as a tool for live performance. The workshops taught software Hydra and Strudel to explore visuals and music in live coding. 

From this residency we performed together, from scratch at Make it Up club, an avant-garde music improvisation night in Melbourne. 

Further performance recorded alongside the Yarra River. Forever becoming together and in every way, bodies intersecting, interacting and interweaving, a site specific performance.
